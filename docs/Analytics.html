Team 28's Skill Progress

Our team is dedicated to progression so we've decided to keep track of skills we would like to further develop in the MSIS program. All of the skills are tied into each other and if we are lacking in one area, it will affect the others. Therefore, it's important to our group to keep everyone progressing. Each of these skills will be monitored over the course of our semester.

Soft Skills

The MSIS program is a technical program so we don't want to lose track of developing our soft skills. These skills could ultimately be what puts us ahead of our peers in terms of job positions.

Team Skills

We will be working with our teams throughout the entire semester and in order for us to succeed we need to have strong team skills. Many of these skills deal with leading, a skill many of us have not practiced, so we will be targetting that primarily.

Technical Skills

Developing our technical skills is a large priority for our group. We understand that programming is essential in the future. Developing our technical skills is a large reason behind our enrollment in the MSIS program.

Interpersonal Skills

Interpersonal skills play a crucial part in our group dynamic. We come from all different cultures so it's important to understand what non-verbals we may be giving off depending on who we are approaching. Our diverse groups gives us a perfect opportunity to develop these skills.

Overall Team Peformance

Lastly, we'd like to monitor our team's peformance on projects. Naturally, we understand that this line may not always go up, but we'd like to finish better than we started.
